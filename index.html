Welcome to GSIS

Welcome to Good Shepherd International School, one of the best basic schools in Ghana, located in the New-Gbawe Community in the capital, Accra. Our core mission is to lay good foundation for moral, academic and spiritual development of our students for lifelong learning and living. We do this by working on their heads, hearts and hands.

Founded on the 22nd of September, Good Shepherd International School has provided, and continue to provide, quality education for the people of New Gbawe Community and the the Greater Accra as a whole.

Our vision is to build a world class school whose products will be tomorrow's leaders of integrity. We believe that education is a process of developing the God-given talents of the child, hence we make sure lessons are learner centered as required by the central body of education in the Ghana Educational Service.

You will find more history of the school in the about section of our website. Kindly feel free to contact us with any concerns or questions you may have. Thank you for visiting us today.
